Welcome to

Rajuk College Science Club

Rajuk College Science Club (RCSC), based at Rajuk Uttara Model College in Bangladesh, is the country’s largest and most renowned science club. It fosters a culture of innovation and creativity, offering students opportunities to explore and develop their scientific talents. RCSC recently hosted the nation’s largest science festival, RCSC National SciSpark 3.0, with the slogan “Grab beyond the infinity,” inspiring young minds to push boundaries in science and technology. The club serves as a hub for nurturing the next generation of inventors and innovators.
